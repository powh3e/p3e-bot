1. You buy into the contract by purchasing P3E, every token purchased increases the P3E price by +0.00000001 ELLA.
2. 10% of what you spend to buy the P3E gets distributed proportionally to all other P3E holders.
3. When you sell your P3E, you get back ELLA at the specified price, and the global price per P3E goes down by -0.00000001 ELLA.
4. 10% of what you would get back for the sale is distributed to all other P3E holders proportionally.
